About


Parthojit Chakraborty is currently working as a PhD candidate in the Department of Information and Communications Engineering at Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan. He received his M.E. degree from the Department of Computational Intelligence and Systems Science, Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan in 2017 and B.E. degree from the Department of Instrumentation and Electronics, Jadavpur University, India in 2015. His interests include the fabrication of nanomaterial composites using atomic metal for gas sensor applications
